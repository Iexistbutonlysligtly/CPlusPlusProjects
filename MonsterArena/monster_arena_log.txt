Racial Abilities:
-----------------
Enrage
-------
 Restores 20 health and increases strength by 1. Cooldown 3.

Charm
-------
Increases the opponent’s cooldown timers by 2. Cooldown 4.

Drain
-------
Deals damage equal to the ghoul’s dexterity and regains half as much health.Cooldown 4.


Professional  Abilities:
-----------------
Cleave
-------
 Deals strength+dexterity damage. Cooldown 3.

Rush
-------
Deals 2*dexterity damage. Cooldown 4..

Bash
-------
Deals 2*strength damage. Cooldown 4.
